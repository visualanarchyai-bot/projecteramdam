Frequently Asked

Shipping & Delivery

We ship our pieces nationwide across India. Orders are processed within 24 hours of payment confirmation. Standard transit time is 4-7 business days depending on your location.

Sizing & Fit

Our pieces are designed with a modern, slightly relaxed streetwear fit. We highly recommend checking the detailed Size Guide available in each product's details before ordering.

Payment Process

All transactions are handled securely via direct UPI when you connect with us on WhatsApp. Cash on Delivery (COD) is not supported for these limited runs.

Returns & Policy

Due to the extreme scarcity of these drops, all sales are final. Returns are only authorized in the rare event of a manufacturing defect upon arrival.
